### The Process
- First we brainstormed ideas to find something that's related to UN Sustainable Development Goals and AIESEC. We thought about projects
that focused on educating people about SDG and projects that focused on helping people figure out what kind of volunteering opportunity
is right for them to motivate people to volunteer. At the end, we landed on the decision of making a game that combines two of these ideas.
- We wanted to how off what kind of things someone from our line of work would do. AIESEC's goal is to send interested volunteers all over the
world and create cross-cultural exchange experiences that develop leadership in young people.
- We started designing some initial wireframes to visualise our ideas. Since we didn't have much time, we moved on pretty qwuickly once we had a
good enough design in our heads.

<img width="815" height="813" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/1ad2e8ad-b23a-4a35-b23c-60fbdcbc025a" />

- We decided to use React for its already available frameworks and plugins. We looked up ways to visualise and interact with the map and found out
that using a SVG would be relatively easy to implement and use (even though it took us a while to get it working initially).

## Requirements 
- Map
- A list of volunteers
- A list of opportunities
- Timer 
- Every x second a country flashes red
- Player has to click on the opportunity, read the description, assing a volunteer that fits best
- Score system
